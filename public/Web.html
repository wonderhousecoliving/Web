The definitive coliving experience

Welcome to Wonder House Coliving, a unique rural coliving space nestled in the heart of Montseny Natural Park. Located just one hour from Barcelona, with frequent direct trains connecting you to Barcelona city center and even the airport, Wonder House offers both tranquility and convenience. At Wonder House, you'll find a place to reconnect with yourself and like-minded individuals, enjoying authentic community living in a stunning natural environment.

Less networking and more not-working

At Wonder House, you can definitely get work done—there's plenty of space and quiet moments for that—but it's not our main focus. We're here to slow down, share experiences, and embrace living fully. Because life at Wonder House isn't about working harder; it's about living better. It's about closing your laptop when a spontaneous hike calls your name, spending lazy afternoons in a hammock, laughing until your sides hurt during game nights, and creating memories with people who value joy and presence above productivity. Here, we remind you that life isn't a to-do list—it's something to savor.

The place is so great that is a mara-villa

A beautifully restored rural villa with over 700m² of comfortable indoor spaces, thoughtfully designed to balance relaxation and productivity. Enjoy a spacious living room and dining area perfect for community meals, an expansive coworking space ideal for focused work sessions, and even your own cinema room for cozy movie nights. Outdoors, the two barbecues, stunning gardens, and refreshing natural pool invite you to unwind, socialize, and reconnect with nature. It's the perfect setting to both work effectively and truly enjoy the peaceful rhythm of life.

The experience

Imagine waking up surrounded by mountains, sipping your morning coffee as sunlight pours through the trees, and starting your day with a deep breath of fresh Montseny air. At Wonder House, life flows differently — you work with focus, share long communal dinners with people from all over the world, and end your evenings laughing around a fire or dancing barefoot under the stars. It's a place where slow living meets deep connection, and every day feels like both a retreat and an adventure.

FAQ

What does my stay at Wonder House include?

Your stay includes accommodation, participation in daily activities, full access to common areas, outdoor spaces, and high-speed internet connection.

What types of rooms are available?

We offer both private and shared rooms suitable for various budgets and stay preferences.

Can I come alone?

Absolutely! Wonder House is specially designed for solo travelers who wish to connect and share enriching experiences with others.

Are pets allowed?

Unfortunately, pets are not permitted at Wonder House. We appreciate your understanding.

What is the minimum stay?

The minimum stay is 1 month, ensuring you fully enjoy the spirit and activities of the coliving experience.

Where can I buy food or basic products?

You'll find local shops, markets, and traditional stores nearby to purchase fresh food and essential products.
