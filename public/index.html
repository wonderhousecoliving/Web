The definitive co-living experience

Just an hour from Barcelona, hidden within Montseny Natural Park, Wonder House isn't your typical co-living space, it's your new playground. Wonder House is all about spontaneous adventures, unexpected friendships, and unforgettable moments. Here, you're not just renting a room; you're joining an offbeat community that values connection, creativity, fun and the occasional trip down the rabbit hole.

Less networking and more not-working

At Wonder House, you can definitely get work done: there's plenty of space and quiet moments for that. But life is so much more than that. Wonder House thrives on spontaneous hikes, quirky games, themed parties you'll still talk about months later, and conversations around the bonfire that go deep into the night. Come for the place. Stay for the people. Leave with memories you'll never forget.

FAQ

Co-living? What the heck is that?

Think of co-living as the joyful antidote to our hyper-connected yet oddly disconnected world. It’s a lifestyle where people actually talk instead of text, cook together instead of ordering solo takeout, and swap endless scrolling for epic adventures. Basically, it's about sharing a home with amazing humans who might start as strangers but quickly become your tribe, creating friendships, memories, and maybe even a little bit of delightful chaos along the way.

How big is the house and the community?

Wonder House has 9 rooms and can host up to 22 amazing humans, but usually, we’ll keep it a bit more intimate. The house itself is a massive rural masia, with enough shared spaces to lose yourself: 2 kitchens for culinary masterpieces, a 24/7 co-working zone, a cozy dining hall, a living room with a fireplace, a cinema room for late-night marathons, 2 barbecue areas, a wild garden, a natural pool for mermaid practice, a mini forest for secret missions... and a forbidden room we’ll definitely pretend doesn’t exist

SWhat should I expect as a co-liver

This isn’t a hotel, it’s a living, breathing madhouse of imperfect humans, so if you use a common space, clean your mess. If the dishwasher’s screaming for help, save it. Being in the countryside means you'll be vibing with bugs, frogs, and the occasional overly ambitious mosquito. Spot a mouse? Blame the local cats: they unionized, demanded better working conditions, and are currently on indefinite strike. We’ve built the ultimate playground for magic to happen, but it’s up to you and your fellow co-livers to bring it to life: organize wild games, sunrise hikes, karaoke battles, you name it. There will be many things happening, but you’re always free to skip a few, hide away with a book, and recharge your inner energy. And if you hear unsettling sounds from the neighbor’s house? Don’t worry. It’s just part of the Wonder House folklore.

What does my stay at the Wonder House include?

Your stay comes with a cozy room/bed to call your own, full access to our common spaces (perfect for spontaneous hangouts and hammock naps), daily activities to spark your spirit, endless outdoor adventures, and a fast Wi-Fi for when you actually need to adult. We host themed communal dinners from Monday to Friday (for a small extra fee), a daily excuse to gather, feast, and connect like family. Trust us, you won't want to miss it. We’ve also got you covered with fresh bed linen, fluffy towels, toilet paper, and a stash of kitchen basics like coffee, tea, oil, and spices to fuel your creations. Laundry’s available too, for a tiny extra charge. Basically, you get everything you need to live, connect, and create some seriously unforgettable moments.

How do I get to the Wonder House?

By plane: Barcelona El Prat Airport is just 1h away by car. There's also a direct train from the airport to Hostalric (the closest village) 2-3 times a day. We can pick you up from the train station. The closest Airport is Girona-Costa Brava Airport, which is 30 minutes away by car.

By car: Just follow Google Maps: Wonder House Coliving, we are less than 1h away from Barcelona and you can park your car at the premises.

By train: There are regular trains from Barcelona city center to the closest village Hostalric (approximately every 2h and the train takes 1h) and a train from Barcelona Airport to Hostalric 2-3 times a day. We will pick you up by car at the Hostalric train station, it’s a 10 min ride from there to the Wonder House.

Crawling: Lost a leg (or two) in the zombie apocalypse? No problem. It'll only take you around 60 hours to crawl your way to the Wonder House. We’ll be waiting at the door armed with axes, chainsaws, and a hot cup of coffee.

What are the working conditions?

When duty calls (or your boss does), we've got your back: a 24/7 dedicated co-working space with ergonomic chairs and beautiful views to keep the inspiration flowing. Prefer a more laid-back vibe? You’ll find plenty of cozy nooks around the house, and even your room comes with its own desk for those "do not disturb" moments. Our Wi-Fi covers the entire house and garden, with speeds over 100 Mbps for downloads and 10–20 Mbps for uploads. And if it ever lags... just shake your fist at the sky and blame Elon Musk.

How do groceries work?

The nearest supermarkets are just a 10-minute drive away. We organize daring grocery quests 2-3 times a week, and if you prefer the wizardry of the internet, you can summon your supplies online right to our doorstep. For our epic communal dinners, we’ll handle the necessary ingredients, you just show up hungry and ready for the feast.

What is the minimum stay duration?

Our minimum stay is 1 month, because real connections don’t happen overnight. It gives you time to settle in, sync with the community vibe, and truly live the Wonder House experience.

What is the cancellation policy?

We ask for 30% of the total amount when you book your stay. If life throws you a curveball and you cancel more than 30 days before your arrival, no worries: you’ll get a full refund. If you decide to leave earlier once you're here, you'll be charged for the next 14 days (or less if your stay ends in less than 14 days). Why? Because in a co-living space like this, every spot matters, and sudden goodbyes leave a ripple in the community (plus, it’s hard to find a new adventurer on super short notice).

What can I do around here?

Montseny Natural Park is basically your giant backyard, packed with endless hikes, secret gorges, and if you're feeling very zen some good old-fashioned mushroom hunting. We're also just 30 minutes from the Costa Brava’s crystalline beaches, surrounded by charming villages, ancient castles, and enough medieval fairs, witch festivals, and local markets to make you wonder what century you woke up in. Need a city fix? Barcelona’s just an hour away and Girona is even closer at 40 minutes. Oh, and if you’re craving adrenaline: trail running, zipline parks, and via ferratas are basically calling your name. Adventure, culture, and a little bit of magic, it’s all waiting for you.

I don't speak any Spanish or Catalan, is that a problem?

No problemo, amigo! English is the main language at the Wonder House, and we make sure everyone’s in the loop. If a conversation drifts into Spanish, Catalan, or Martian, we gently hit the "switch to English" button so no one gets left out.

Are pets allowed?

Furry friends are welcome in the apartment for a small extra fee! Just a heads-up: we already have a few unofficial, self-appointed garden guardians (a.k.a. the local cats) who like to chill in the sun but politely keep their paws out of the house.

Can I come with children?

Not for now. Wonder House is currently an adults-only playground, partly because we host some slightly wild, grown-up activities that don’t exactly scream "kid-friendly." We might revisit this policy in the future, but for now, it’s all about the adults getting to be kids again.
